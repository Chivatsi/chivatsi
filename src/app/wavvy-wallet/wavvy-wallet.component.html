Project Challenge

How might we build a smart and personalized recommendation system that predicts and organizes your personal, business and group expenses providing users with informed decision making abilities about their finances?

Project Goals

Wavvy Wallet targets different user groups i.e. personal / individuals, business and groups / chamas. These different user groups have different goals and needs.

Goal #1:Link transactions to an account i.e. personal, business and groups.

Goal #2:Import different transaction types from your SMS messages such as MPesa, MShwari or bank SMS messages.

Goal #3:Auto-track expenses based on categories, sub-categories and tags.

Goal #4:Provide direct savings / investment opportunities directly through the mobile application.

Goal #5:Recommend targeted alternatives and suggestions to increase revenues/ reduce costs.

Goal #6:Understand your financial position by analyzing loans and setting spending limits.

Goal #7:Generate different user reports depending on target customers such as profit/loss statements, expense reports, spend/person and many more.

Goal #8:Understand different user needs and create relevant, targeted marketing campaigns such as bulk SMS & social media advertising avenues.
