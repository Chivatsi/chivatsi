Our Challenge

How might we provide accurate and real-time broadcast monitoring logs to musicians in Africa in order to offer transparency in the royalty collection and distribution process?

Project Goals

Request Africa is a music analytics company focussed on emerging markets that has devised new technological strategies to ensure artists wholly receive royalties owed to them.

Goal #1:Creating an efficient lean ecosystem around artists and IP right-holders in Africa.

Goal #2:Furnishing stakeholders with accurate and reliable information that lead to agile decision making and financial success.

Existing Solution

Each society is required to file an annual report and copies of audited accounts.

All the three CMOs are also mandated to distribute at least 70% of the revenue
they collect plus submission of their yearly budgets to curb on wastage.

Why It Doesn’t Work

Difficult to implement without a streamlined digital system

Expensive for upcoming artists to get registered as members

Lack of transparency

Request Africa’s Solution

Integrate automated delivery of real-time logs via whatsApp and
a web dashboard to maximize on convenience and prompt delivery.
WhatsApp
A chat companion that provides insights from an artist’s music performance on terrestrial radio via Whatsapp.

Web Dashboard

A web dashboard that provides in-depth insights to artists on how their music is being consumed on terrestrial media

Membership System

A web dashboard that allows for registration of new members and upload of music
